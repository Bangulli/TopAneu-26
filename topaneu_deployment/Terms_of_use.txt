Specification of the terms of data use of the TopAneu 2026 challenge released data:

* Following the definitions of "https://opendata.swiss/en/terms-of-use" on open data use, the following license is chosen:

"Terms of data use:

    Open use. Must provide the source. Use for commercial purposes requires permission of the data owner.

        * You may use this dataset for non-commercial purposes.

        * You may use this dataset for commercial purposes, but you must seek prior permission from the data owner.

        * You must provide the source (author, title and link to the dataset)."

The data owners are the institutions contributing data to the TopAneu 2026 challenge, including:

    * Lausanne University Hospital, CHUV, Switzerland
    * Geneva University Hospitals, HUG, Switzerland
    * University Medical Center Utrecht, UMCU, The Netherlands
    * Mie Chuo Medical Center, Japan

The resulting publications or pre-prints of the TopAneu 2026 challenge will serve as reference for data citation.

By downloading the data, users agree to these terms of data use.
